The game starts here
Torontos First Esports conference Frame Freeze is an annual conference/tournament that aims to bring together coaches/recruiters from universities across Ontario as well as students in both high school and university to learn more about Esports and compete in a simulated professional tournament. We plan to bring keynote speakers to inspire our participants to make their dreams come true.
Passon Rules! Our mission is to provide aspiring individuals with opportunities to pursue a career in the Esports industry. Frame Freeze will be a bridge between potential employers/coaches and their employees/players. On top of this, we want everyone to have a chance at doing what they love! Passion goes very far in life and this is what we want our participants to share with the community!
